Lingotek Document Portal

Technology

A service built to integrate with Lingoteks' translation service. It's purpose was to allow for
project managers/admins/translators to manage projects and see live updates on translation jobs.
This project had already been built out but while I was here I was the sole and lead developer on this service.

I had a hand in facilitating it's development and then release process. One of which was rewriting part of the back-end architecture
to closer match with the companies other services using apache2 instead of nginx. This process included lots of experience
working with docker containers as well as working with HAProxy.

In addition to this, I was also at times, tasked with working on 3rd party integrations for lingotek, mostly within Magento and Confluence.
